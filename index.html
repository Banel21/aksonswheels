About Aksons Wheels Durban

Your Trusted Automotive Partner in eThekwini

Aksons Wheels Durban is a newly established automotive dealership strategically located in the heart of eThekwini Municipality. As a proprietary limited company registered under South African law, we are dedicated to providing affordable, reliable, and fuel-efficient vehicles to the Durban community.

Our mission is to be the premier provider of mobility solutions in Durban, building trust and loyalty through exceptional customer service and a commitment to transparent, value-driven transactions. We specialize in compact cars, light commercial vehicles, and motorcycles - the fastest-growing and most resilient segments in the South African market.

With over three decades of combined experience in the South African automotive sector, our leadership team brings intimate knowledge of local market intricacies, consumer psychology, and operational excellence to every aspect of our business.
